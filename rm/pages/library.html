📥 Install the McGhee Lab Capture Chrome extension
Captures papers from publisher pages and PDF tabs straight into the lab library.
  1. Click Download extension below → unzip wherever you keep dev tools.
  2. Open chrome://extensions/, flip Developer mode ON (top-right).
  3. Click Load unpacked → select the unzipped extension/ folder.
  4. Pin the M icon in the Chrome toolbar (puzzle-piece menu → pin).
  5. Click the icon → Sign in with your @arizona.edu Google account.
